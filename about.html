About Us

Abhilash Raghavendra is a qualified Astrologer and Vastu consultant practising for more than 20 years. He is a professionally certified consultant who also uses his intuition to offer guidance to the clients, encouraging them to move in a positive direction and face the challenges that may lie ahead of them. He strongly believes that Astrology is a tool that reveals many aspects of our character and destiny. Moreover, it gives us an insight of the likely outcome of our actions and barriers we face in our lives. The remedies suggested by him have logical background and are very easy to follow which does not disturb the regular schedule. His predictions also include Favourable and not so favourable timelines for his clients to be aware. It could be with respect to health, Wealth, Investment or Work. More than 20,000+ clients have taken advantage of the Astrological as well as Vaastu Consultations which includes Politicians, Actors, Directors, IT Professionals, Bank Officers, Businessman from around the globe. When you schedule your Horoscope reading or Vaastu Consultation with Abhilash Raghavendra, you will receive a detailed and highly accurate reading that covers the aspects of your life where you feel you need the most guidance.
