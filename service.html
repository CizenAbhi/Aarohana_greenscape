Our Services

Astrology Consultation

Astrology consultation provides personalized guidance by analyzing the positions and movements of celestial bodies at the time of your birth. It offers insights into various aspects of your life, helping you understand your strengths, challenges, and potential future events.

Gemstone Consultation

Gemstone consultation involves recommending specific stones based on your astrological chart to enhance well-being and balance energies. These tailored recommendations aim to address personal challenges and promote positive influences in your life.

Gemstones

In astrology, gemstones are believed to harness the energy of specific planets and can be used to enhance positive influences or mitigate negative effects. Each gemstone is associated with a particular planet and is worn to attract beneficial energy and improve aspects of one's life.

Spell

A spell is a ritualistic set of actions or words believed to harness magical powers to achieve a specific outcome. Spells are often used in various spiritual and magical traditions to influence events, bring about desired changes, or protect against harm

Vastu Consultation

Vastu consultation involves analyzing and advising on architectural and design elements of a space according to traditional Indian principles to harmonize energy flow and enhance well-being. This practice aims to align living or working environments with natural forces to promote health, prosperity, and peace.

Vastu Planning

Vastu planning involves designing and arranging buildings in harmony with natural forces to enhance health, prosperity, and well-being. It incorporates principles of direction, layout, and spatial geometry rooted in ancient Indian architectural science.
