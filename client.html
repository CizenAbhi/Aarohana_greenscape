The Navaratnas

Ruby Gemstone

Ruby gemstone is traditionally associated with passion, courage, and energy, making it suitable for enhancing vitality, assertiveness, charisma, and influence, particularly favoured by leaders, creatives, and those seeking to amplify their presence and impact in various aspects of life.

Pearl

Pearl, an organic gemstone, is utilized as a remedy for a weak moon in Vedic Astrology, enhancing self-confidence. It is recommended for those grappling with mental stress, as it is believed to promote mental peace, reflecting the human mind like the Moon. Beyond its material form, the pearl is revered as a reservoir of positive emotions and goodwill.

White Sapphire

White sapphire, also known as Shweta Pushyaraga, is a precious gemstone revered as an effective remedy for the planet Venus. If your horoscope indicates a strong Venus but you're not experiencing desired outcomes related to marriage, relationships, or pleasure, wearing White Sapphire is recommended. Governed by Venus, it is associated with enhancing the benefits related to marriage, sexual life, and overall pleasure.

Blue Sapphire

Blue Sapphire gemstones have been valued since ancient times for their multifaceted uses. Saturn, known as a karmic planet associated with diligence and perseverance, can be fortified by Blue Sapphire, addressing potential challenges in health, wealth, personal life, and professional career stemming from its weak placement in a horoscope.

Yellow Sapphire

Yellow Sapphire also known as Kanaka Pushyaraga, is governed by the planet Jupiter, renowned for its auspicious and beneficial influence. It is believed to bestow upon the wearer good health, wisdom, wealth, longevity, fame, and protection from malevolent forces. Enthusiastically wearing Yellow Sapphire is said to bring forth these significant benefits to the individual.

Emerald

The Emerald gemstone is a miraculous aid for those struggling with concentration issues, particularly benefiting students by enhancing intelligence. Emerald soothes mental agitation, regulates the nervous system, alleviates nerve pain, and enhances speech. It promotes healing, revitalizes breathing, strengthens the lungs, and fosters mental flexibility and adaptability.

Coral

Coral, an organic gemstone ruled by the planet Mars, is considered a powerful astrological remedy in Vedic Astrology. Mars, symbolizing courage and confidence, is believed to impart enhanced self-assurance to those who wear red coral, enabling them to navigate challenging situations with agility and determination.

Gomed

The Gomed gemstone is renowned for mitigating the adverse effects of Rahu while enhancing its positive attributes. Its popularity stems not just from its mystical appearance but also from its profound healing capabilities. Gomed soothes the wearer's mind, alleviating depression, deep-seated anxieties, and mental issues. It aids in improving concentration, enhancing focus, and providing clear direction towards achieving goals.

CatsEye

The Cat’s Eye gemstone is renowned for lessening the malefic effects of ‘Ketu’ while amplifying its positive attributes. Its popularity is not only due to its mystical appearance but also because of its enigmatic healing properties. The Cat’s Eye is believed to attract luck and enhance the wearer’s intuitive and spiritual powers. It aids in healing emotional wounds and sparks creativity.
